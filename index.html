How it works

Guided startup assembly

Founder input moves through classification, module selection, expert review, operations runtime, and capital readiness — one structured path.

Classify

Start with a wizard: what you're creating, ownership structure, domain, and launch model. Service, product, platform, marketplace, agency, software, AI, and more.

Assemble

Compatible meta-modules are selected automatically. Each module brings ready-to-use expert knowledge — inputs, outputs, constraints, and validation rules.

Compile

The Startup Compiler produces your full package: positioning, naming, brand, product structure, architecture, cloud servers, API integrations, UI applications, legal drafts, pitch structure, and runtime workflows.

Verify

Verifier agents check assumptions, compatibility, legal risk, financial sanity, technical feasibility, security, and need for expert review. Experts step in when human judgment is required.

Run

Operations Runtime executes business processes: lead capture, sales, payments, onboarding, support, and continuous operations — from day one.

Grow

Evolve modules, pricing, and workflows while operations continue. Prepare for capital access. Market feedback closes the loop.
